Brief introduction of myself

Hi, I am Paul Barrère, a fluid dynamics enthousiast and an astrophysics passionate. Since October 2024, I started a postdoctoral postion at the Observatory of Geneva (Switzerland), where I mostly work on the impact of magnetic instabilities and dynamo action in stellar interiors.

In this personal webste, you can read a short description about me and my research, or download my CV.

Feel free to get in contact regarding any questions or collaborative suggestions.
